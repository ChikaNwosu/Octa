As you go through life you see, there is so much that we don't understand. And, the only thing we know is things don't always go the way we planned. But you see everyday whe things never go your way, and it seems all your dreams come undone, we will stand by your side, filled with hope and filled with pride, for we are more than we are, we are one. We are one body, we are one. If there's so much I must be, can I still just be me, the way I am.
